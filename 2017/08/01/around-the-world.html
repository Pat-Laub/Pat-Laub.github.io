Around-the-world conference trip

Montreal, Barcelona

Aug 1, 2017

I travelled to the International Conference on Monte Carlo Methods and Applications in Montreal, and the Foundations of Computational Mathematics conference in Barcelona. Both were amazing, though a month of travelling was slightly exhausting. Definitely check out the photos I took. A huge thanks to the UQ Graduate School for funding the trip through their Candidate Development Award, and to ACEMS for covering the conference fees!
