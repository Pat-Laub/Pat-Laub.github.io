About Me

Hi, I’m Pat Laub. I’m a mathematician & software engineer currently finishing a PhD in applied probability theory. Other areas I’m interested in are Monte Carlo methods, finance, computational/experimental maths, machine learning, statistics, big data, economics, artificial intelligence, computer science, history, international relations, public health, genetics, books. I have extensive experience in software roles including work at Google & NICTA, and am a long-serving tutor at UQ. For more background you can check out my LinkedIn profile.
